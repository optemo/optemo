- There is a hero product at the top
- There are 3 product categories displaying 3 products each

- Filter for red cameras by clicking on red color swatch and check if only red cameras are displayed
- repeat the previous step for other colors
- filter brands by clicking on one and multiple brand check boxes and check if right products are displayed

- Click on the next page link; Go back

Product Comparison:
